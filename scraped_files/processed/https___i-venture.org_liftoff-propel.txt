About Liftoff Propel 



Liftoff Propel is a 3-month accelerator program designed jointly by Wandhwani Foundation and I-Venture @ ISB, to support MVP stage startups from T1/T2/T3 cities, with a market-tested MVP and early or pilot customers, interested in securing PMF and validating their business model.This accelerator program will be tailored to startups’ needs and equip them with a refined product pitch, scalable business tools, resources for comprehensive market validation, and funding assistance. 











Who is eligible? 







Registered StartupWith minimum 1 year from incorporation. 







MVP StageMarket-tested with early or pilot customers

FAQs 



 






Who is eligible to apply for the Liftoff Propel Program?

Startups incorporated for a minimum of 1 year, at the post MVP stage or advanced product stage (TRL 8-9), and in the early revenue stage with scalability potential are eligible to apply.







What support does the program offer to selected startups?

The program offers comprehensive support, including mentorship from ISB & Wadhwani foundation, tailored masterclasses and workshops, business development and market access assistance, access to lab facilities, go-to-market strategies, scale-up support and investor connect.







What is the duration of the program?

The program has a timeline of approximately 8-10weeks,.It also includes a Demo Day event for startups to showcase their progress and accomplishments.







What happens after the application submission?

After the application submission, the shortlisting and onboarding process will take place. Shortlisted startups will be notified and provided with further instructions and details regarding their participation in the program.







How can I get in touch for further inquiries?

For any further inquiries or questions, please feel free to contact us at nagaraj_bolakatti@isbdlabs.org / ankit.machhar@wfglobal.org  Our team will be happy to assist you and provide the necessary information.
