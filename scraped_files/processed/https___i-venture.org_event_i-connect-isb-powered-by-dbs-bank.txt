I-Connect @ ISB Powered by DBS Bank

July 25, 2022 @ 10:30 am - 8:00 pm 






I-Hustle @ ISB: Pitch Day at MFABulous 2022 »










I-Venture at ISB is back with the second edition of I-Connect @ ISB powered by DBS Bank, this time in MoTown!
I-Connect @ ISB is being hosted to facilitate networking opportunities within the entrepreneurial ecosystem and to create a platform where like-minded entrepreneurs can share ideas and meet new people. This would be a special occasion for the AIC ISB Association too, as we are set to inaugurate our AIM Makerspace on the same day in the afternoon.
An exciting opportunity to meet and network with the who’s who of the startup ecosystem.
This is an invite-only event. You can register on this link: https://lnkd.in/d5EcyRqe
Once you register, we will send you the invite based on the availability of slots.
