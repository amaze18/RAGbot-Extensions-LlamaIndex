Web3 enthusiasts and entrepreneurs in and around, step in for an unmissable evening of learning at the 3rd monthly edition “Web3 in India – Navigating the Ecosystem” of #WEB3Wednesdays (W3W) powered by Metabuild.
Bringing enthusiasts, builders, entrepreneurs, professionals, and early-stage investors under one roof for a high-intensity dose of industry insights and cross-learning opportunities.
Let’s experience an ambitious, community-driven platform that brings web3 to the masses, exploring its opportunities, challenges, use cases, and impact.
