Netherlands: Your Gateway to the World

December 14, 2022 @ 11:00 am - 1:00 pm

I-Venture @ ISB in collaboration with the Netherlands Point of Entry is organizing a session on “Netherlands – Your Gateway to the World”.
If you want to work or start a business in the Netherlands, this session will guide you through the “why and how” of getting started. Additionally, you will learn about the many opportunities available in the Netherlands.
The Netherlands Point of Entry is the first stop for international talent looking to work or set up a business in the Netherlands.
 
Date: December 14, 2022
Venue: AC2 NMLT, Level-2, Main Building ISB, Hyderabad campus
