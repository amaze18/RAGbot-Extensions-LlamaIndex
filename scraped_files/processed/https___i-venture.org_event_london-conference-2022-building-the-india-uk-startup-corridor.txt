London Conference 2022: Building the India-UK Startup Corridor

October 27, 2022 @ 2:00 pm - 9:00 pm 





« I-Venture @ ISB celebrating its one year anniversary
TechSparks 2022 | 13th Edition | YourStory »










I-Venture @ ISB in collaboration with the Wheeler Institute for Business and Development at London Business School is hosting the London Conference 2022, to foster deeper collaboration between two leading startup ecosystems – India and the UK.
The conference aims to bring together startups and key ecosystem players from both ecosystems on a single platform to facilitate cross-talk, collaboration, growth and expansion opportunities in both countries, thereby contributing to strengthening India-UK relations.
The conference will be held at The North Building, London Business School on October 27, 2022.
