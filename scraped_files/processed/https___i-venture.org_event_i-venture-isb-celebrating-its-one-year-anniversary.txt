I-Venture @ ISB turns One!
It warms our hearts to share that I-Venture @ ISB has completed one year of its existence. Thank you all for being a part of the I-Venture @ ISB family throughout. This very special milestone couldn’t have been achieved without your support and presence at every turn.
The I-Venture @ ISB team is delighted to invite you to our first anniversary celebrations as we embark on a grand journey of many more milestones.
Join us to commemorate this very special anniversary! Know more
Date: 24th September 2022
Time: 7 pm onwards
Venue: Mirror Pool, ISB, Hyderabad campus
