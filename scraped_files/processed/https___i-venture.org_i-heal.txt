Program Details 



The I-HEAL @ ISB is a flagship healthcare accelerator program by AIC-ISB as part of I-Venture @ ISB focusing on last-mile healthcare delivery in the country and scaling innovative solutions to make healthcare accessible and affordable for all.In our efforts to support breakthrough healthcare innovations by startups in India, the program will guide the startups, having at least a tested prototype, and accelerate their journey through product engineering and development, clinical validation support, deep need-based mentorship, regulatory advisory, market access, fundraising opportunities and much more! 







EligibilityThe product/service should be at or above MVP stage (Technology Readiness Level 5 or more)Commercially viable and has the potential to create an impact

FAQs 



 






Who is eligible to apply for the I-HEAL@ISB 2.0 Acceleration Program?

The startups having their product/service at or above MVP stage (Technology Readiness Level 5 or more)Commercially viable and has the potential to create an impact







What support does the program offer to selected startups?

In our efforts to support breakthrough healthcare innovations by startups in India, the program will guide the startups, having at least a tested prototype, and accelerate their journey through product engineering and development, clinical validation support, deep need-based mentorship, regulatory advisory, market access, fundraising opportunities and much more!







What happens after the application submission?

After the application submission, the shortlisting and onboarding process will be completed by Feb 2024. Shortlisted startups will be notified and provided with further instructions and details regarding their participation in the program.







Is there any Program Fee/Equity/Success Fee associated with the participation?

There is a component of Program fees involved in the Acceleration phase of the Program. There is a nominal fee of INR 20,000 + GST which the selected startups must pay before the commencement of the Acceleration Phase.
