About the Program 



 DLabs Incubator Association (DLabs) at the Indian School of Business (ISB), in collaboration with Broadridge, launches a global accelerator program for start-ups working in AI, Blockchain and DeFi space. The two organizations have come together to support and nurture innovations by offering comprehensive support, including mentorship, capacity building and networking opportunities. We empower startups working in different themes to thrive and attract the attention of relevant stakeholders and investors. 










Criteria 





Startups working in AI, Blockchain and DeFi thematic areas 





Minimum 1 year from incorporation 





Early revenue stage ventures with scalability potential 













Themes 





 



Use of AI in testing 





 



Generating insights from account and market information 





 



Foundational technology and infrastructure for Blockchain and DeFi ecosystems 







 



Integration of AI and Blockchain technologies in fintech 





 



Developing innovative decentralised applications on Blockchain platforms in fintech

FAQs 



 






Who is eligible to apply for the FinChain @ ISB powered by Broadridge Acceleration Program?

Startups working in AI,  Blockchain and DeFi thematic areas, incorporated for a minimum of 1 year, at the post MVP stage or advanced product stage, and in the early revenue stage with scalability potential are eligible to apply.







What support does the program offer to selected startups?

The program offers comprehensive support, including mentorship from Dlabs and Broadridge, tailored masterclasses and workshops, business development,  go-to-market strategies, scale-up support, internationalisation support and strategic partnerships.







How long does the FinChain @ ISB powered by Broadridge Acceleration Program last?

The program has a timeline of approximately 9 weeks, including the acceleration period from January to March followed by a Demo day.







What happens after the application submission?

Shortlisted startups will be notified and provided with further instructions and details regarding their participation in the program.







Is the FinChain @ ISB powered by Broadridge Acceleration Program open to startups outside of India?

Yes, startups across the globe can apply







How can I get in touch for further inquiries?

For any further inquiries or questions, please feel free to contact us at contact@isbdlabs.org Our team will be happy to assist you and provide the necessary information.




















I-Venture @ ISB is a brand umbrella bringing together all the pillars of entrepreneurship at ISB - DLabs, AIC ISB, DIRI, and TRICOR under one roof with an aim to foster entrepreneurship and nurture startups.
