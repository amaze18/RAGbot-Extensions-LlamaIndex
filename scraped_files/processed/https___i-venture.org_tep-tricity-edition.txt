About the Program 



 The Technology Entrepreneurship Program  – Tricity Edition aims to provide an enabling platform that is conducive for aspiring and budding student innovators in the Tricity (Mohali, Chandigarh & Panchkula) region and provide access to mentorship and support in ideation, innovation, prototyping, and fundraising which contributes to their entrepreneurial journey.

FAQs 



 






How to register for the roadshows?

The host institutions will be sharing registration forms and further information on the program with the students.







What are the criteria for forming a team?

Minimum 2 members and Maximum 4 Members should be there in each team. Students from different academic years can also form a team together.







Is there any application fee?

No application fee is involved in any phase of the program.







What happens after the application is submitted?

Once the application deadline is reached, all the submitted applications will be internally evaluated by the I-Venture @ ISB team. Shortlisted ideas will be notified and provided with further instructions and details regarding their participation in the next phase of the program.







What are the criteria on which the applications will be shortlisted for the next phase?

The applications will be shortlisted based on Technical Feasibility, Market Awareness, Value Proposition, and Scaling Potential.







What will happen to the applications which are not shortlisted for the next phases?

The potential ideas which are not shortlisted will be shared with our pool of ecosystem partners and enablers. They will then onboard the teams with respect to their preference and help them in their product development journey.







Will the shortlisted ideas receive funding from I-Venture @ ISB?

The shortlisted teams will be taught about different funding opportunities available to the students and the I-Venture @ ISB team will help them to write for these grants. Also, these teams will be recommended by us to our partnered organizations who fund early stage innovators.




















I-Venture @ ISB is a brand umbrella bringing together all the pillars of entrepreneurship at ISB - DLabs, AIC ISB, DIRI, and TRICOR under one roof with an aim to foster entrepreneurship and nurture startups.
